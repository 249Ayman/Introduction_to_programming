How Online Courses Are Changing Learning: A Transformative Shift

The COVID-19 pandemic has reshaped education worldwide, leading to a surge in online learning. Here’s how this shift is revolutionizing the way we learn:

Continue reading...

GMA News Hub

What'e E-learning

January 1, 2024 by Ayman

The closure of physical schools led to an unprecedented surge in e-learning. Over 1.2 billion children worldwide adapted to remote teaching via digital platforms. Research suggests that online learning enhances information retention and often proves more efficient in terms of time.

Beyond the Classroom

Online courses transcend geographical boundaries. Students can access top-tier institutions and learn from experts regardless of their location. This democratization of education opens up new possibilities for global learning.

EdTech Boom

Even before the pandemic, the edtech industry was booming. In 2019, global edtech investments reached $18.66 billion. Language apps, virtual tutoring, video conferencing tools, and online learning software witnessed exponential growth during the crisis.
