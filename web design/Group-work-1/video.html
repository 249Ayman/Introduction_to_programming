Grammar with Kevin: Unconventional Linguistics

  • Instructor: Kevin Malone
  • Office Hours: Whenever the vending machine is restocked
  • Location: Conference room B (next to the break room)
  • Prerequisites: A love for chaos and a basic understanding of vowels
  • Course Code: LING-404

Course Description:

Are you tired of those uptight grammar rules? Do you find yourself stifled by the shackles of conventional language? Fear not! In this groundbreaking course, we'll dive headfirst into the delightful abyss of linguistic absurdity. Kevin Malone, your unorthodox language guru, will guide you through a syllabus that defies all norms. Buckle up, because we're about to shred the dictionary and redefine syntax!

Course Objectives:

  1. Banana Phonetics (Week 1): - Explore the subtle nuances of banana-based communication. - Learn how to convey complex emotions using only the word "banana." - Master the art of elongating syllables for dramatic effect (e.g., "ba-naaa-naaa").
  2. Office Jargon Simplification (Week 2): - Why say "synergy" when you can say "teamwork magic"? - Decode Dwight's cryptic memos and transform them into relatable haikus. - Bonus: Crafting passive-aggressive sticky notes for the break room fridge.
  3. The Art of Abbreviation (Week 3): - "LOL" is for amateurs. We'll invent our own acronyms like "BANANA" (Brilliantly Abbreviated Nonsensical And Nearly Absurd). - Explore the delicate balance between brevity and utter confusion.
